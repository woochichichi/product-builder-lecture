What is MBTI?

MBTI (Myers-Briggs Type Indicator) is a personality type assessment developed based on Carl Jung's psychological types theory. It combines 4 preference indicators to classify personalities into 16 types.

E Extraversion vs I Introversion

Energy direction: Whether you gain energy from the outer world or the inner world

S Sensing vs N Intuition

Information perception: Whether you prefer concrete facts or patterns and possibilities

T Thinking vs F Feeling

Decision making: Whether you prioritize logic and objectivity or values and relationships

J Judging vs P Perceiving

Lifestyle: Whether you prefer planned and systematic or flexible and spontaneous
